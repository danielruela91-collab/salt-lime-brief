The Offer

$1 margaritas for mom — dine-in only, May 10th

Redemption: show a photo of your kids on your phone. Easy, warm, no friction. Make this explicit in every piece of content so guests know exactly what to expect before they arrive.

Strategy — two audiences, two jobs

Existing followers

Email + SMS

Goes to people already in the list. Job is to remind regulars and bring them back in on May 10. They know Salt & Lime — they just need the nudge.

New follower acquisition

Social

Reaches people outside the list. CTA drives them to sign up as a VIP on the website. Once they sign up, the offer lands automatically via the welcome email.

See post → Sign up as VIP → Get offer by email → Redeem by May 10

Deliverables

Email

1 dedicated Mother's Day email — existing followers

Hero visual with $1 marg offer front and center. Call out the photo-proof redemption clearly. CTA = come in May 10, dine-in only. Tie in "Moms, Dads & Grads" gift cards if space allows.

Subject line
"Mom deserves a $1 marg (we're serious) 🌿"
Body note to include
"Just show us a photo of your kids when you arrive — that's all the proof we need. First margarita is $1, on us."
SMS

1 Mother's Day SMS — existing followers

Short and urgent. Redemption mechanic in the copy. Send 1–2 days before or day-of May 10.

SMS copy
"Mom gets $1 margaritas this Sunday at Salt & Lime 🌮 Show us a photo of your kids. Dine-in only, May 10."
Social

1–2 posts — new follower acquisition

Different job from email/SMS — drives people to sign up as VIPs on the website to unlock the offer. CTA is "sign up to get the deal," not just "come in." Post ASAP — more time live = more sign-ups before May 10. Reel idea: moms showing off their kid photos at the bar to claim the deal.

Caption idea
"Moms eat (and drink) free-ish this Mother's Day 💚 Sign up as a VIP at saltandlimeaz.com to get the exclusive offer straight to your inbox. $1 margaritas, dine-in only, May 10. All locations."
